# **App Name**: Study Buddy AI

## Core Features:

- Content Analysis: Analyze provided text and extract key concepts and relationships.
- Question Generation: Generate practice questions, including multiple choice and short answer, based on extracted key concepts. The questions will be crafted to align with various learning levels and test different comprehension skills.
- Performance Feedback: Assess user responses to generated questions and offer personalized feedback. This includes highlighting correct answers, identifying areas for improvement, and offering explanations to clarify difficult concepts. The tool may consider the correctness and completeness of the response in offering suggestions.
- Content Input: Intuitive interface for uploading or pasting text content.
- Question Display: Clear presentation of generated questions and answer input fields.
- Progress Visualization: Progress tracking and visualization of user performance over time.
- Session Management: Save and retrieve previously analyzed content and question sets.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4B0082), chosen for its association with knowledge and wisdom.
- Background color: Light lavender (#E6E6FA), a desaturated tint of indigo to provide a calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Violet (#8F00FF), for a contrasting but harmonious highlight that adds a touch of energy without disrupting the calm.
- Headline font: 'Literata', serif, chosen for its literary, intellectual associations.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, chosen for its clean legibility to complement the headline font.
- Use minimalist icons to represent content types and actions.
- Clean and organized layout with a focus on readability and ease of navigation.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and provide feedback on interactions.
